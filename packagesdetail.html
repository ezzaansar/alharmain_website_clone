Itinerary

Makkah: 4 Nights Madina: 3 Nights

Day 1: Arrival at Jeddah Airport

Our driver will pick you from Jeddah Airport and drop you at Makkah Hotel Infinity Hotel Emaar Andalusia. Check-in at Emaar Andalusia and you will stay here for 4 nights.

Day 2-4: Makkah

Perform Umrah and visit the significant Ziyarat sites in Makkah.

Day 5: Going to Madina

Our Driver will pick you from Makkah Hotel Emaar Andalusia and drop you at Madina Hotel Jawaharatot Al Rasheed.
Check-in at Jawaharatot Al Rasheed Hotel. Visit Masjid al Nabawi at your preference.

Day 6-7: Madina

Ziyarat tours in Madina and enjoy your stay.

Day 8: Departure from Saudia

After Check-out from Madina Hotel Jawaharatot Al Rasheed, our driver will pick you and transfer to Jeddah or Madina Airport. Departure back to the home country.
